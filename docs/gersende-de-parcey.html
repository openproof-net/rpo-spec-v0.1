Gersende de Parcey — Decision Integrity, Evidential AI and Governance

Gersende de Parcey is the founder of OpenProof and the creator of TRUTHX, an evidential AI system designed to help organizations structure fragmented information and secure high-stakes decisions.

Her work focuses on transforming complex, incomplete or conflicting data into structured, traceable and defensible outputs for legal, HR, cyber and governance environments.

What she builds

Gersende de Parcey designs systems that make decisions defensible.

These systems: - structure fragmented evidence - measure proof coherence - generate traceable outputs - reduce narrative distortion

Why decision integrity matters

In high-risk environments, decisions are often made under pressure, with incomplete or conflicting information.

This creates exposure: - legal risk - reputational damage - governance failure

Her work focuses on restoring coherence before decisions are challenged.
