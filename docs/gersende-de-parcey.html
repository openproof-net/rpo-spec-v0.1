Gersende de Parcey. Decision integrity. Evidential infrastructure.

Gersende de Parcey is the founder of OpenProof and the creator of TRUTHX, a decision integrity and evidential infrastructure designed to help organizations structure fragmented information, stabilize traceability, and defend high-stakes decisions.

Her work focuses on legal, HR, cyber, compliance and governance environments where decisions may later be challenged, audited, exposed or contested.

Why decision integrity matters

High-stakes governance
What usually fails
Exposed decisions rarely fail because there is no information. They fail because evidence is fragmented, ownership is unclear, definitions drift and narrative pressure outruns traceability.
What that creates
Legal vulnerability, governance fragility, reputational damage, institutional escalation and weak executive defensibility.
Strategic thesis
Before an organization can defend a decision, it must first defend the structure of the information on which that decision was made.
