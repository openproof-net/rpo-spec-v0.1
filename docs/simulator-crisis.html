Which stories compete?

Dominant narratives are hypotheses, not conclusions.

Compatible with the financial target. Does not explain the ignored operational dependency.

Coverage: partial · one contradiction

Compatible with the omission. Decision rights and information access remain unresolved.

Coverage: incomplete · causal attribution open

Compatible with fragmented ownership, missing map and deadline pressure.

What becomes defensible?

The system does not decide guilt. It distinguishes what can be supported, what remains uncertain and what should happen next.

Supported

  • Financial pressure existed.
  • The formal decision chain operated.
  • Dependency information was incomplete.

Not yet supported

  • Intentional concealment.
  • Individual causal responsibility.
  • That another option would certainly have succeeded.

Next robust action

Reconstruct the dependency map, decision rights and information availability before assigning responsibility or redesigning the programme.

Human decision

Board, counsel or investigator decides using the reconstructed record and their professional mandate.
