1. Do not confuse title with power

Formal authority may exist without the corresponding operational means.

2. Do not confuse legal separation with autonomy

Separate legal entities may be economically or technically inseparable.

3. Do not confuse succession with causality

Arriving before the rupture does not prove causation.
