Try the RPO Playground — from narrative to draft proof bundle

Paste a short narrative, add a few identifiers, and generate a draft RPO JSON bundle with heuristic scores and a public hash. This page demonstrates how the RPO standard can structure a story into something verifiable.

Experimental
Deterministic
Open source friendly
This is an open playground — no AI model, no registry, no legal value. It is here to show how an RPO bundle could look and behave, before plugging advanced engines or CNRS modules.
What this page does
Local only
It takes your narrative and turns it into a draft RPO bundle:
  • Computes basic metrics (length, structure, evidence density).
  • Builds a minimal rpo.json object.
  • Derives a public_hash from the full content.

Think of it as an RPO sandbox: the “code civil” exists, this page shows how a simple engine could apply it to real narratives.

How this page works

        

          

            

              This open playground shows how a simple narrative can become a
              minimal RPO bundle. Everything runs locally in your browser:
              no backend, no registry, no AI model. It is a didactic PMVP, not a legal tool.
            

            
    
              
  • You write a narrative and a few labels (issuer, subject, title).
    • 
              
  • The page computes three heuristic signals: sentences, evidence markers, coherence score.
    • 
              
  • A minimal rpo.json object is generated, plus a public_hash in SHA-256.
    • 
            

            

              The goal is to make the structure of proof visible: what today is “just a story”
              becomes a bundle that can, tomorrow, be anchored, versioned, and interpreted
              by more advanced engines.
            

          

          

            

              In the scientific CNRS × TruthX pilot, this skeleton will be
              enriched with psycho-forensic heuristics: interpretive coherence, patterns of
              coercive control, inverted narratives, and probatory coherence scores.
            

            

              This open version deliberately stays simple and transparent so that anyone can
              inspect the JSON, recompute the hash, and reuse the bundle in their own tests
              or tools.
            

            

              Open playground = no AI, no central registry, no legal value. Scientific pilot =
              advanced psycho-legal engines, controlled environment, and strict governance.
