Open standard for HR decision governance

HR decisions will be challenged. Make them auditable and defensible.

OpenProof RPO v0.1 turns HR and governance decision trails into a deterministic proof bundle: signed JSON, human-readable PDF, and a public hash. No AI. No interpretation. Humans remain accountable.

Same input → same output. Deterministic structure, designed for audit, compliance and legal readiness.

Integrity, not “truth”. The hash proves integrity and traceability, not the correctness of the narrative.

Decision lineage you can defend. Source of truth, data lineage, business rules, accountability perimeter.

How it works
Step 1 — Capture

Write the decision context (what was known at decision time). No retroactive reconstruction.

Step 2 — Bundle

Generate a deterministic bundle: signed JSON, PDF rendering, public hash for integrity.

Step 3 — Verify

Anyone can verify integrity and traceability, then audit accountability over time.

About the author

RPO v0.1 was created by Gersende Ryard de Parcey, an HR decision governance architect (Paris). This public sandbox demonstrates how deterministic proof bundles strengthen auditability, data lineage, and defensibility for contested HR decisions—without replacing human judgement.
