Why it matters

Legal exposure
When a decision is challenged, missing context becomes liability. RPO forces explicit scope, sources, and responsibility perimeter.
Audit readiness
Auditors need reconstructible evidence. RPO provides deterministic outputs and a verifiable integrity anchor.
Operational clarity
A “single source of truth” is not a slogan. It is lineage, rules, quality controls, and stable definitions across entities.

How it works

Step 1
Capture decision context
Short narrative + declared sources available at decision time. No retroactive reconstruction.
Step 2
Generate a deterministic bundle
Same input, same output: signed JSON, human PDF rendering, and public hash for integrity over time.
Step 3
Verify, audit, defend
Anyone can verify the bundle integrity, trace what existed, when it existed, and under which accountability perimeter.
