Decision governance — not a reporting tool

OpenProof RPO v0.1 is not a dashboard layer. It is a governance infrastructure designed for environments where decisions may be challenged and context must be reconstructible.

The objective is simple: lock the decision perimeter at decision time, generate deterministic outputs, and preserve human accountability.
