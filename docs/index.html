Why it matters

Legal exposure

When a decision is challenged, missing context becomes liability. RPO forces explicit scope, sources, rules, and responsibility perimeter.

Audit readiness

Auditors need reconstructible evidence. RPO produces deterministic outputs and a verifiable integrity anchor, without rewriting history later.

Operational clarity

“Single source of truth” is not a slogan. It is lineage, rules, controls, stable definitions, and traceable decision-making across entities.

How it works

Step 1 — Capture

Write the decision context, declare sources available at decision time. No data fishing, no retroactive reconstruction.

Step 2 — Generate

Same input, same output: signed JSON, human PDF rendering, and a public hash for integrity over time.

Step 3 — Verify

Anyone can verify the bundle integrity, trace what existed, when it existed, and under which accountability perimeter.
