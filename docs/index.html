HR decisions will be challenged. Make them auditable and defensible.

Why it matters

Legal exposure

When a decision is challenged, missing context becomes liability. RPO forces explicit scope, sources, rules, and accountability perimeter.

Audit readiness

Auditors need reconstructible evidence. RPO produces deterministic outputs and a verifiable integrity anchor — without rewriting history later.

Operational clarity

“Single source of truth” is not a slogan. It’s lineage, rules, controls, stable definitions, and traceable decision-making across entities.

What you will do in the Sandbox
Step 1
Capture the decision context
Write a short narrative and declare what sources existed at decision time. No data fishing, no retroactive reconstruction.
Step 2
Generate a deterministic bundle
Same input, same output: signed JSON, human PDF rendering, and public hash for integrity over time.
Step 3
Verify, audit, defend
Anyone can verify the bundle integrity and trace what existed, when it existed, and under which accountability perimeter.
