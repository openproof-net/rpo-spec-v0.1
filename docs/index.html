Why deterministic (and why no AI)

Same input → same output

If the result changes with every run, you cannot defend it. Determinism is a governance property, not a UX preference.

Integrity over persuasion

The bundle is designed to be verifiable. The hash proves stability of content over time, not “truth” as an opinion.

Humans remain accountable

The standard does not “decide”. It captures perimeter, sources, rules, and uncertainty—so the decision remains explainable under pressure.
